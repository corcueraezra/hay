About Us

"Hayahay" is an obscure word in the Tagalog language, but in the Bicolano language, it means Relaxed, Comfortable, Cool & Refreshed.
In Hayahay Travels and Tours, you’ll be travelling with ease and relaxation.

Hayahay Travels and Tours is a travel agency duly-licensed by the Department of Tourism. We are a full service travel and tour agency, this means that regardless of your vacation needs we can help. We provide a wide array of services including international and local packages comprising of land arrangement, airline tickets, land transfers and sightseeing tours. The company operates with our very own Tour Van and friendly driver for local area tours.

Hayahay Travels and Tours is also a member of the prestigious National Association of Independent Travel Agencies (NAITAS) and the Bicol Association of Travel & Tour Operators (BATTO).
